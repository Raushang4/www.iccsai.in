WIE-IEEE WORKSHOP ON “AI- QUANTUM COMPUTING” IN COLLABORATION WITH QisKit

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is an international community of IEEE members and volunteers committed to supporting female engineers and scientists while motivating young girls worldwide to pursue their academic passions in engineering and science. Our objective is to encourage the inclusion and progression of women in technical fields on a global scale. We envision a dynamic community of both women and men within IEEE, utilizing their diverse skills to drive innovation for the betterment of humanity.

The WIE-IEEE Workshop on "AI-Quantum Computing" is a highly anticipated event aimed at exploring the intersection of Artiicial Intelligence (AI) and Quantum Computing. This workshop provides a platform for researchers, professionals, and enthusiasts to delve into the emerging ield of AI-Quantum Computing and its potential applications. Participants of the workshop will gain insights into the latest advancements in both AI and Quantum Computing, and how these ields can synergistically enhance each other. The workshop will feature renowned speakers who are experts in the respective domains, sharing their knowledge and experiences through presentations and panel discussions. Overall, the WIE-IEEE Workshop on "AI-Quantum Computing" aims to bring together a diverse community of professionals to promote knowledge exchange, collaboration, and innovation in the realm of AI and Quantum Computing. The workshop provides a unique platform to explore the cutting-edge advancements, applications, and implications of these transformative technologies.

Ph.D. SYMPOSIUM

The IEEE PhD Symposium is an annual event that brings together doctoral students, academic faculty, and industry professionals to share research findings and discuss emerging trends in various ields of engineering, computer science, and technology. The event includes keynote speeches, panel discussions, technical paper presentations, and networking opportunities. Its goal is to provide a platform for PhD students to showcase their research, receive feedback, and interact with experts in their ields. It's a valuable event for those looking to advance their research, build their professional networks, and prepare for successful careers in academia or industry.
